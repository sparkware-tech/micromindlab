Who We Are

Building Future Innovators

At MicroMind Lab, students explore creativity, technology, innovation, and hands-on learning in an exciting modern environment.

We focus on practical experiences that help students think creatively, solve problems, and build real-world projects using modern technologies.

From engineering concepts to digital creativity and innovation, students develop future-ready skills through interactive learning and experimentation.

  • Hands-on learning and creative exploration
  • Innovation, and problem-solving skills
  • Real-world projects and future-focused activities

The Learning Path

Four Stages to Innovation

Foundation Stage

The Starter

Building essential digital skills. Students learn to use computers effectively and understand the basics of technology.

Stage 1 · Beginner

The Explorer

Building real circuits from day one. Students learn to ask "how does this work?" and find the answer by making it.

Stage 2 · Intermediate

The Maker

From circuits to machines. Students build robots that move, sense, and respond — writing real code to control them.

Stage 3 · Advanced

The Innovator

Internet of Things and smart systems. Students build devices that can be controlled from a smartphone, from anywhere in the world.

How It Works

Getting started is simple

1

Free Discussion

A session at your home. Your child builds a real circuit on day one — no prior experience needed.

2

Placement Chat

We find the right stage based on your child's age and interests. Relaxed, no pressure, and no formal tests.

3

Weekly Home Classes

I come to you. Sessions are 2–3 hours, once a week, fully hands-on with real components.

4

Build & Level Up

Each stage ends with a showcase project your child presents. Then they move on to the next stage.
